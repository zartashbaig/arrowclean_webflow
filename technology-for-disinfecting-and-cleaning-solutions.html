ArrowClean's patented device produces both TK60 One-Step Disinfectant™ and FC+ All-Purpose Cleaner™ from only two ingredients, water and pure salt. Seriously, that’s it. The process is called "water electrolysis" and we've spent nearly a decade perfecting it. A technology so simple, so SAFE and so highly-effective, it needs to be everywhere!

See How It Works

The Device

ArrowClean gives facilities the power to produce batch after batch of powerful disinfecting and cleaning solutions on-site. The device is easy to install, patented and fully automated, giving you the “Ready-to-Use” (RTU) solutions you want, when you need them. The process uses water, salt and electricity to produce 450 gal. (150/300) of TK60 and FC+ solutions daily.

EPA registered and TUV Safety Certified.

The Solutions

TK60 One-Step Disinfectant kills many pathogens including COVID-19 (Coronavirus) in 20 seconds and MRSA, C. diff spores, TB, and Black Mold in 60 seconds. FC+ All Cleaner safely removes stains, dirt, and grime from surfaces. It also prevents detergent buildup on furnishings and flooring that often dull appearances and trap odor-causing bacteria. Both solutions are conveniently dispensed “Ready-to-Use” (RTU) for use in spray bottles, floor scrubbers, backpack sprayers, etc.
Does not promote the formation of resistant strains of pathogens
Complies with FDA code for use on food contact surfaces with no rinse
Replaces disinfectants, disinfecting wipes, sanitizers, deodorizers, window cleaners
Can be added directly to auto-scrubbers, carpet, and upholstery cleaning equipment
Improves traction (coefficient of friction) reducing risk of slips and falls
Produced at 750 ppm NaOH, FC+ breaks down oils and fats for powerful degreasing action
Replaces all-purpose cleaners, degreasers, carpet cleaners, floor cleaners and grout cleaners
